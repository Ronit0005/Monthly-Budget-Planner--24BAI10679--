**Problem Statement:**\
Managing personal monthly expenses is difficult for many individuals, especially students and working professionals who handle limited budgets. People often do not track where their money goes or how much they should be saving every month. This leads to overspending, low-savings, and poor financial planning. The goal of this project is to create a simple offline Java-based tool that helps user plan their monthly budget, track expenses, and stay aware of their financial habits.

**Scope of the Project:**

The project focuses on building a console-based monthly budget planner. The application provides basic budgeting features without relying on external databases, internet access, or complex UI.

The scope includes:

- Creating local user profiles
- Calculating recommended monthly savings
- Generating a simple budget breakdown
- Recording user expenses with notes
- Allowing users to update income, goals and profession
- Saving and loading data from text files

Advanced financial analytics, web interfaces, or cloud support are not part of the current project scope.

**Target Users**

The application is designed for:

- Students managing pocket money or hostel monthly expenses
- Hostellers and working individuals tracking food, travel and personal expenses
- Anyone who wants a simple online budgeting tool
- Beginners learning Java who want to understand OOP and File Handling

Since the application is lightweight and runs in a terminal, it is suitable for everyday users.

**High Level Features**

- **Create and manage local profiles:** Each user can store their name, profession, income, savings and goal.
- **Goal Based Savings Calculation:** Monthly savings are automatically computed using goals or default benchmarks.
- **Budget Breakdown:** Food, travel, and personal categories are generated based on income.
- **Expense tracking:** Users can record expenses with amount and note, stored in individual text files.
- **Update functionality:** Users can update their data anytime.
- **Offline storage**
- **Simple console UI**
